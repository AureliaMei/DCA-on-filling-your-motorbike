# DCA on Petrol

## Project Overview
**Repository:** [https://github.com/AureliaMei/DCA-on-petrol](https://github.com/AureliaMei/DCA-on-petrol)

This project explores the economic and temporal trade-offs between two motorbike refueling strategies in Vietnam: **Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)** and **Full Tank**. By simulating fuel consumption and purchases using historical PVOIL price data (2018–2026), the project aims to determine which strategy is more cost-effective and convenient for different types of vehicles.

## Repository Contents

### Data Collection
*   **`00. get gas price.py`**:
    *   **Purpose**: Crawls historical gasoline prices from the PVOIL website.
    *   **Tech**: Uses `playwright` (async) to navigate the site, interact with dropdowns, and scrape pricing tables.
    *   **Output**: Saves data to `pvoil_prices.json`.

### Simulations
*   **`01. full_tank purchase_simulation.py`**:
    *   **Strategy**: "Full Tank".
    *   **Logic**: Refuels to 100% capacity whenever the fuel level drops to 10%.
    *   **Output**: Generates purchase history and total cost analysis for this strategy.

*   **`03. DCA purchase_simulation.py`**:
    *   **Strategy**: "Adaptive DCA".
    *   **Logic**:
        *   Sets a fixed budget (approx. 80% of a full tank).
        *   **Capped Rule**: Lowers the budget if the tank overflows consecutively (due to price drops).
        *   **Short Gap Rule**: Triggers a "Restart" (Full Tank) if refueling frequency becomes too high (due to price hikes).
    *   **Output**: Generates purchase history and total cost analysis for the DCA strategy.

## Key Insights

### Financial Efficiency (DCA Wins)
*   The **Adaptive DCA** strategy consistently saves money compared to the Full Tank strategy.
*   **Savings**: Ranges from **1.71% to 3.92%** depending on the vehicle type (e.g., Sport bikes save more).
*   **Stability**: DCA smooths out monthly expenditure volatility.

### Time Efficiency (Full Tank Wins)
*   The **Full Tank** strategy requires fewer trips to the gas station.
*   **Frequency**: Reduces refueling stops by **20% - 27%**.
*   **Convenience**: Users can go 11-13 days between refills, compared to 6-10 days for DCA.

## Conclusion
*   **Choose DCA** if you want to optimize for cost and "beat the market" during volatile periods.
*   **Choose Full Tank** if you value your time and prefer fewer interruptions.

## Bottom Line: What to do in case of an IRAN WAR? 🌍💥
If geopolitical tensions (like an Iran conflict) threaten to spike global oil prices:
*   **Switch to Adaptive DCA immediately.**
*   **Why?** During periods of high volatility and price shocks (similar to the 2020-2022 data), DCA proved to be the "safest" bet to minimize the impact on your wallet. It smooths out the price spikes and ensures you aren't buying a full tank at the absolute peak.
